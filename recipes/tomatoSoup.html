Tomato Soup Recipe

Tomato Soup — A Comforting Classic Rooted in European Cuisine

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Sauté chopped onion
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Pour broth
  4. Simmer
  5. Blend and serve
